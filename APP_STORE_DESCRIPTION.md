## Full Description

### Description Paragraph

Tarot Deck App brings the ancient wisdom of tarot cards to your mobile device with a modern, intuitive experience. Connect your Solana wallet to purchase credits and unlock personalized tarot readings featuring beautifully rendered cards based on the classic 1909 Rider-Waite-Smith deck. Whether you seek quick daily guidance or deep insights into complex questions, our AI-powered readings provide thoughtful interpretations tailored to your unique situation. All readings are stored locally on your device, ensuring your personal journey remains private and secure.

### Features:

• **Three Classic Tarot Spreads**

- Single Card Reading (1 credit): Quick daily guidance or focused questions
- Past, Present, Future Spread (2 credits): Understand how time influences your situation
- Celtic Cross Spread (5 credits): Deep, comprehensive 10-card reading for complex questions

• **AI-Powered Interpretations**

- Receive personalized narratives that weave together card meanings
- Context-aware readings that consider your question and card positions
- Detailed meanings for love, health, career, and spirituality

• **Beautiful Card Design**

- Authentic tarot imagery based on public-domain 1909 illustrations by Pamela Colman Smith and Arthur Edward Waite
- Smooth card reveal animations and intuitive interface
- Support for both upright and reversed card meanings

• **Solana Wallet Integration**

- Secure payment processing through Solana blockchain
- Simple credit-based system for easy access to readings
- No personal information required—just connect your wallet

• **Reading History**

- Access your past readings anytime
- Readings stored locally on your device for privacy
- Review insights and reflect on your journey

• **Privacy-First Design**

- All reading data stored locally on your device
- No account creation or personal information required
- Wallet connection optional—use the app with just your device

### About Data:

**Privacy & Data Collection:**
Tarot Deck App is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

• **What We Collect:**

- Wallet address (only if you connect a Solana wallet)
- Reading data stored locally on your device
- Device information (type, OS version) for app functionality
- Transaction data from Solana blockchain (publicly available)

• **What We DON'T Collect:**

- Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contacts or other device data
- Biometric information

• **Data Storage:**

- All reading data is stored locally on your device using secure storage
- Wallet addresses stored locally for app functionality
- No data transmitted to external servers except for payment processing
- You can disconnect your wallet or delete the app at any time

• **Your Rights:**

- Access your stored data anytime
- Delete all data by uninstalling the app
- Disconnect your wallet through app settings
- No account required—complete control over your data

For complete details, see our Privacy Policy: https://etolopez.github.io/tarot-deck-app/PRIVACY_POLICY.html
