# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Tarot Deck App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Wallet Address**: When you connect a Solana wallet, we store your wallet address to associate credits and readings with your account.
- **Reading Data**: We store your tarot readings locally on your device and optionally associate them with your wallet address for retrieval across devices.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, and reading history
- **Transaction Data**: Solana payment transactions (publicly available on blockchain)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contacts or other device data
- Biometric information

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide Services**: Process payments, grant credits, and deliver tarot readings
- **Improve App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- **Security**: Verify transactions and prevent fraud
- **Support**: Assist with technical issues and user inquiries

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

- Reading data is stored locally on your device using secure storage
- Wallet addresses are stored locally for app functionality
- Credit balances are stored locally and associated with your wallet address

### Blockchain Data

- Solana payment transactions are recorded on the public Solana blockchain
- Transaction data is publicly visible but not personally identifiable

### Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:
- Payment processing (Solana blockchain)
- Analytics (if applicable)
- Error tracking (if applicable)

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Secure local storage using encrypted storage mechanisms
- Secure wallet connection protocols (Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter)
- No transmission of sensitive data over insecure networks

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request access to your stored data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your local data by uninstalling the app
- **Disconnect**: Disconnect your wallet at any time through app settings
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app at any time

## Wallet Connection

When you connect a Solana wallet:

- We request authorization to read your wallet address
- We do NOT request permission to send transactions without your explicit consent
- All transactions require your approval through your wallet app
- You can disconnect your wallet at any time

## Payment Information

- Payments are processed through the Solana blockchain
- We do NOT store payment card information or banking details
- Transaction details are publicly available on the blockchain
- We only store transaction signatures for credit verification

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Providing in-app notification for significant changes

## Data Retention

- **Reading Data**: Stored locally until you delete the app or clear app data
- **Wallet Address**: Stored locally until you disconnect your wallet
- **Transaction Data**: Permanently recorded on Solana blockchain (public record)

## International Users
